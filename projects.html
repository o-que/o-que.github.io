ASRA

is a digital artist, designer, and developer exploring the range of her artistic and technical practice. Her areas of focus include: generative art, design, web development, and more recently, motion graphics and videography. The post-pandemic world’s new need to recreate physical experiences in the virtual world has motivated her to begin exploring 3D and virtual spaces. As a natural continuation of her previous work, she has taken an interest in Creative AI, cellular automata, and other procedural artwork. ASRA wants to explore creative possibilities of algorithmic systems. Her work plays with structure, movement, distortion and vibration to produce kaleidoscopic and psychedelic imagery. She will receive her BFA in Digital Media Art from San José State University in Spring 2021.
